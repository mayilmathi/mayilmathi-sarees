About Us

A brand-new beginning, woven with intent.

Mayilmathi Sarees has just opened its doors. We're not a big warehouse or a mass retailer — we're a small, new label built one order, one conversation, and one saree at a time. Because we're just starting out, our stock is deliberately small and constantly changing, which means the sarees you see today may not be here tomorrow.

What we promise, even this early: every order is tracked carefully from the day it's placed to the day it reaches your door, and every piece of feedback — good or growing — genuinely shapes what we bring in next.
